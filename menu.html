Coffee

Turkey: Türk Kahvesi

Served in a copper pot, the Türk Kahvesi has a foamy texture which you may enjoy! Watch out though, this coffee does not contain cream nor milk. We also add cardamom for that kick while drinking it!

Price: 125 Pesos

Sweden: Kaffeost

A niche product we create is called the Kaffeost. It is a Swedish and Finnish drink wherein we put Leipäjuusto cheese cubes in coffee, yes, cheese!

Price: 150 Pesos

Extra Cheese: +20 Pesos

Greece: Frappe

I'm sure we're all familiar with what a frappe is. A cold beverage made out of instant coffee (who knew!?), water, sugar, and foamed milk, plenty of foamed milk. We serve them in 3 flavors: plain, chocolate, and caramel!

Prices: Plain - 120 Pesos || Chocolate - 130 Pesos || Caramel - 130 Pesos

Chocolate Chips: +20 Pesos || Whipped Cream +15 Pesos

Mexico: Café de Olla

Brewed inside of a claypot, we add unrefined cane sugar, orange peel, cloves, allspice, and cinnamon to give the coffee flavor.

Price: 125 Pesos

Vietnam: Ca Phe Da

Brewed through a French drip filter into a cup of ice mixed with condensed milk. This drink is extra sweet and extra strong!

Price: 150 Pesos

Condensed Milk drip: +20 Pesos

Tea

India: Golden Chai

Developed almost 5,000 years ago, the Chai Tea has become a staple drink around the globe, and has landed here in our cafe! Buy one now!

Price: 100 Pesos

China: Jasmine Green Tea

The Jasmine Green Tea combines the Jasmine flower buds and the Green Tea leaves to create an aromatic experience like no other.

Price: 100 Pesos

Taiwan: Gunpowder Green Tea

Each individual leaf is rolled up into a ball, similarly like a grain of gunpowder. Each leaf is withered, steamed, rolled, and then dried protecting the tea from damage and allowing it to retain its flavour and prevent oxidation.

Price: 100 Pesos

Egypt: Chamomile Tea

Chamomile is made from a daisy-like plant which serves various medicinal purposes and offers a warm, earthy, floral experience.

Price: 100 Pesos
